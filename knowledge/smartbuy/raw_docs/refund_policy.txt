SmartBuy Refund SOP

1. Verify order ID and customer identity before any refund action.
2. Refund eligibility is 7 calendar days from delivery for damaged or wrong items.
3. For non-delivery complaints, check courier scan status and raise logistics ticket.
4. If refund is approved, create refund request in OMS and share SLA of 5 business days.
5. Escalated dissatisfied customer must be offered supervisor callback in under 4 hours.
